Did You Know?

Tacos originated in Mexico and date back to the 18th century. The word "taco" comes from the Nahuatl word "tlahco" meaning "half" or "in the middle."

Taco Varieties

There are over 50 different types of tacos in Mexican cuisine, each with unique ingredients and preparation methods specific to different regions.

Corn vs Flour

Traditional Mexican tacos are made with corn tortillas, while flour tortillas became popular in northern Mexico and the southwestern United States.
